Uses IBW / AdjBW if TBW >120% IBW.

Exclusion / caution for extended-interval

Hartford-type extended-interval regimens are typically NOT used if CrCl <30 mL/min, in pregnancy, or in complex PK situations (burns, CF, ascites, synergy).
